Users can directly view their information through a verification procedure every quarter, and after that, they can request access to their information by phone, in writing, or by email to the person in charge of the relevant notice.

The procedure for filing an objection when rejecting a data subject's request for access, correction, deletion, or processing suspension is as follows:

① When the personal information management department postpones or rejects a request for access, etc., it notifies the legitimate reasons and the method of objection within 10 days from the date of receiving the request.

② The personal information management department accepts and receives the data subject's objection application (in writing, by phone, by email), and the personal information manager confirms the content.

③ The personal information management supervisor conducts a final review of the processing results.

④ The personal information management department notifies the data subject of the processing results.

This website takes the following technical measures to ensure security so that users' personal information is not lost, stolen, leaked, altered, or damaged when handling it:

Anti-virus programs are updated regularly, and in the event of a sudden virus outbreak, the vaccine is provided as soon as it becomes available to prevent personal information from being compromised.
